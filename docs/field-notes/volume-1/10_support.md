If this volume was useful to you, here are four ways to support it.

1. **Star the repo** at <https://github.com/Jthora/HelmKit>. Costs you
   nothing; helps the project find the people it is for.
2. **Tip the work** at <https://ko-fi.com/helmkit>.
3. **Join the interest list** to be notified when the Tier 1 DIY kit and
   Tier 2 hand-built units open for order.
4. **Commission a custom build**, starting at $5,000 with an 8–12 week
   lead time. Built to your spec on the open HelmKit platform.

The HelmKit roadmap walks four generations of frame and three commercial
tiers in parallel. This chapter is the public version of that roadmap.

## Generations

| Gen | Posture | Status |
|-----|---------|--------|
| Mk0 | Cosplay-grade frame, hardpoints stubbed | shipped |
| Mk1 | First measure-and-modulate revision | in design |
| Mk2 | RF + sister-module integration | concept |
| Mk3 | Consumer-grade DIY kit | concept |

## Commercial tiers

| Tier | Product | Window |
|------|---------|--------|
| Tier 1 — Field Notes Vol. I | $9 + PWYW digital PDF | June 2026 |
| Tier 1 — DIY Build Kit | $35 digital (files + BOM + guide) | August 2026 |
| Tier 2 — Hand-Built Unit | $500 deposit | Sept–Oct 2026 |
| Custom Commission | $5,000+, 8–12 wk lead | always open |

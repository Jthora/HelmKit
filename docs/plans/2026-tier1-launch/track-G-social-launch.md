## Goal

Convert existing audience attention (TikTok demo video already posted, X following, Patreon list) into:

1. Ko-fi tips (revenue, ~$5–$50 range).
2. Field Notes commissions (revenue, $9 PWYW).
3. Interest-list signups (zero-cost re-engagement funnel for future Tier 1 DIY / Tier 2 / commission pre-launch).
